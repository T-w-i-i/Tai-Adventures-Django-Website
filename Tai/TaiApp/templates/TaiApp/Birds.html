Arrival in Nairobi
The safari will start immediately you land at the airport where you are picked up and dropped at your hotel of choice, you will have a rest as you feel the beautiful ambiance of the famously known City under the Sun. Briefing with the guide who will share information and familiarizing with what to expect during the safari. Nairobi excursion offered upon request.

DAY 1: NAIROBI – HELLS GATE NATIONAL PARK
Safari starts early after breakfast where you will be picked up from the hotel by our drive guide as you head for Naivasha through the city in sun displaying it architectural designs from the ancient Asian design, European design to the modern sky scrapper along the highway. With variety of flora and vegetation from cash crops to staple crops indicating the economic activities in the region, short stop over at the Fly Over View Point for the beautiful scenic view of the Rift Valley that cuts through Kenya. Arrive at Hells Gate National Park through the Elsa Gate. The park is favorable for biking in the wild, climbing the fishers’ tower and drives. Go through the gorges such as the Ol Norway gorge and caves spotting wildlife along the way like the warthog, zebra, giraffes and different species of birds at times one of the big cats can be spotted roaming. The park is famous for inspiring the Awarding Winning Musical Walt Disney Film The Lion King, which got its concept from the park. With the Ol Karia Geothermal Station inside the park, its boost of being one of a kind in Africa and has a spa pool reputed to be effective in therapeutic. NB: Bikes and climbing gears are for hire and one has to request for these to be organized upon arrival. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 2: NAIVASHA – LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK
Immediately after breakfast head for Nakuru approximately 1 hour 30 minutes’ drive, Lake Nakuru National Park is dubbed a world heritage site and also an important birding area nesting various birds ranging from the blue eared starling, lesser and greeter flamingoes, great white pelican, hottentot teal, marabou stork among others. Visit the pink covered lake from millions of flamingoes which come to feed from the algae in the perfect alkaline lake. The park is home to wildlife ranging from the indigenous black rhino, non-indigenous white rhinos, Rothschild giraffe, lions, zebras, leopards, baboon, monkeys, impalas and hyrax. With some amazing sites such as the lion hill, baboon cliff, and makalia falls inside the park offers good experience for watching wildlife, scenic viewing and have some picnic. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 3: NAKURU –LAKE BOGORIA NATIONAL RESERVE
Wake up to the sweet melodies of the different bird species within the range of the park, an early breakfast after which we shall head for Lake Bogoria which is also an important birding area, world heritage site and ramsar site being a favorite haunt for the lesser flamingoes. Has the highest concentration of geysers in Africa with the hot springs making it an attraction worth a visit. Nesting various birds such as the lesser flamingo which feed on the blue green algae, greater flamingo feeds on lake flies, the crimson birds and wildlife such as the Greater Kudu and African wild dogs. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 4: LAKE BOGORIA – LAKE BARINGO
Drive through the rough rocky terrain after breakfast as we enter the neighboring Lake Baringo an Important Birding area and ramsar site and a haven for bird lovers ranging from flamingoes, storks, pelicans, spoonbills and the Goliath Heron which is the largest heron on earth. The hunting African fish eagle displays its prowess as it easily scoops fish out of the water, crocodiles and hippos are seen with the help of an amazing experience of the boat ride upon request. Scenic view of the Laikipia escarpments and the Tugen Hills offers an amazing display of the amazing surrounding around the lake which had fluctuated a few years back to the great submerging of all the buildings around it after flooding from heavy rains in the region. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 5: LAKE BARINGO - NAIROBI
Early game drive followed by late breakfast with check out from the lodge as we head for Nairobi arriving late in the afternoon where you will dropped off at hotel of choice or airport for departure as this marks the END of our safari. Thank you for choosing to travel with us.
Meal Plans: (Breakfast & Lunch)
