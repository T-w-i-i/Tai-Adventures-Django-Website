Nairobi being the capital city also boost as the only City in the world with a National Park, it has a lot to offer for tourist who are on transit and would like to start their safari in the city or those in the city for a short stay (business). This is the only safari that you will get a chance to be closest to wild animals than any other due to the variety of attractions that you will visit.

Departure
Safari starts from your hotel after breakfast where you will be picked up by our driver guide, the city is usually busy in the morning and hence you get to experience the rush within the city and the different architectural designs of buildings covering the city.

Day 1
Hotel - Nairobi National Park
The park is situated approximately 7 Km from the City ,while in the park the sky scrapers can be seen from a distance covering the sky .It is home to some of the big 5 ( lion, buffalo, rhino and the leopard) , other animals in the park includes the giraffe, zebra, hyena, cheetahs, gazelles, waterbucks etc. Being a bird area you will get to come across birds such as the vultures and ostrich which can be seen on sight. Since the park is small in size you get to enjoy the early game drive with a good proximity of wildlife.
Visit to David Sheldrick Elephant & Rhino Sanctuary
At around 1100 hours, a visit to David Sheldrick sanctuary which is situated in the park and its home to adopted elephants that are rescued from poachers who kill herds for tusk. They are nursed here and taken care off since most of them are usually traumatized from the acts of the poachers. The sanctuary is one of the best places to come close to a calf and feed it directly creating that special moment and bond with the largest mammal on earth.
Visit to the Animal Orphanage
In the afternoon you visit the animal orphanage within the park where you get to see some wild animals such as the lion, cheetah, hyenas, leopards, baboons and various birds. The orphanage is a rehabilitation Centre and treatment facility for wild animals that have been saved from the wild either with injuries or from natural occurrences.
Nairobi safari Walk
The final activity for the day will be the amazing safari walk on a wooden bridge that stretches across the park and allows one to get a glance of various wild animals in the park. During the walk one can get a chance to see the white rhino, zebras, antelopes, monkeys and baboons including other wildlife. The walk is usually breathtaking and it’s the only place one can get to walk in a park for game viewing hence the best experience in the park.
Embark to your hotel for dinner & overnight

Day 2
Hotel - Bomas of Kenya
Pick up from hotel early after breakfast, for the best cultural experience a visit to bomas of Kenya will leave you anxious and anticipating for more due to diverse and rich cultural practices. Different showcase of traditional attire, dances and practices ranging from the Masaai Dance, Giriama Songs, Kikuyu tales, Luo naming and Luhya traditional rites of passages will leave you thrilled. The magnificent display of traditional homestead in form of Manyattas, thingiras (huts), and how the huts where built around the homestead with specific positioning of huts will be explained. Get a chance to enter the traditional manyattas which are built using mud, cow dung and covered with grass rooftops you will seat around the traditional 3 stones fire place which used to be the kitchen and fire place. The diverse culture in Kenya is well represented here and your visit here will leave you wanting to learn more of it.
Bomas of Kenya – Giraffe Centre
Giraffe Centre is approximately 40 minutes from the City Centre and is home to the Rothchild Giraffe, being the tallest land mammal and an endangered species the conservancy rescues and rehabilitates giraffes. Have an amazing chance for memorable photos while feeding the giraffe from your hand with its long rough tongue stretching for the bites. You create the best photo album with the giraffe since they are social and friendly and the best highlight of the safari will be in the center as you end the tour.
Drop off to the hotel or airport as that will be the end of the City safari.
