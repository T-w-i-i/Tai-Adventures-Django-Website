Arrival in Nairobi

The safari will start immediately you land at the airport where you are picked up and dropped at your hotel of choice, you will have a rest as you feel the beautiful ambiance of the famously known City under the Sun. Briefing with the guide who will share information and familiarizing with what to expect during the safari. Nairobi excursion offered upon request.

DAY 1: NAIROBI – MASAI MARA GAME RESERVE
Departure starts from your hotel straight after breakfast where you will be picked up by our driver guide as you head for Masaai Mara through the Nairobi – Mai Mahiu highway. Along the way you will get to see more of the change in social settings from urban to rural settings including change in terrain and flora. Pass through the Old Roman Catholic Church which was built by Italians along the highway with your first stopover at the view point for great scenery view of the famous Rift Valley that cuts through Kenya. With the change in terrain the land is filled with wheat and barley which is planted in plenty in Narok, arrive in the reserve late in the evening for hotel check in and evening game drive in search for game including the big 5 (Buffaloes, Elephants, Leopards, Lions and Rhinos) and various wildlife.
Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 2: MASAAI MARA FULL DAY DRIVE
Immediately after breakfast the search for big game in the famous reserve starts at dawn, the reserve is home to more than 1.3 million wildebeest which usually migrate to the Serengeti National Park making it the seventh wonder of the world. With plenty of prey ranging from zebras, antelopes, gazelles, topi, impalas make it favorable for hunting and kill by predators such as black maned lions, leopards, cheetahs and other carnivores such as hyenas, foxes, wild dogs, jackals and many others. Large herds of elephants and giraffes are a spectacular scene that you will get to experience iced with the beauty of the blacked rhino, with the big 5 roaming around your day will end with a memorable scene.
Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 3: MASAAI MARA RESERVE – LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK
Early after breakfast have a morning game drive with the melodies from birds filling the reserve which is an important birding area for species such as the secretary bird, ostrich, kori bustards, marabou stork, vultures and plenty others. Other wildlife within the reserve includes warthogs, hippopotamus and crocodiles within the famous Mara River where the migration takes place. Embark to the lodge for check out as we head for Nakuru where we shall arrive late in the evening for check in. Within the park various wildlife ranging from the indigenous black rhino, non-indigenous white rhinos, Rothschild giraffe, lions, zebras, leopards and other wildlife can be spotted.
Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 4: LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL RESERVE FULL GAME DRIVE
Being an important birding area you will wake up to the sweet melodies of various birds ranging from the blue eared starling, lesser and greeter flamingoes, great white pelican, hottentot teal, marabou stork among others. Morning game drive after breakfast having a visit the pink covered lake from millions of flamingoes which come to feed from the algae in the perfect alkaline lake. The park is home to wildlife like the baboon, monkeys along the bushy trees, impalas, hyrax, and zebras. The park has amazing sites such as the lion hill, baboon cliff, and makalia falls inside the park for viewing and scenery watching.
Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 5: LAKE NAKURU – NAIVASHA
Check out from the lodge after breakfast heading for Naivasha roughly 1 hour 30 minutes’ drive as we enter the Hells Gate National Park through the Elsa Gate. The park is favorable for biking in the wild, climbing the fishers’ tower and drives as you go through the gorges such as the Ol Norway gorge and caves spotting wildlife along the way such as the warthog, zebra, giraffes and different species of birds and at times one of the big cats is spotted roaming. The park is famous for inspiring the Awarding Winning Musical Walt Disney Film The Lion King, which got its concept from the park. With the Ol Karia Geothermal Station inside the park, its boost of being one of a kind in Africa and has a spa pool reputed to be effective in therapeutic. NB: Bikes and climbing gears are for hire and one has to request for these to be organized upon arrival.
Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 6: NAIVASHA – AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK
Immediately after breakfast head straight for Amboseli National park passing through Nairobi, enjoy the scenic view along the highway with the different types of settlements ranging from different cultural practices. Arrive in Amboseli late in the afternoon for check in and a late game drive after a shortly relaxing from the long drive. The park is home to the big 5 expect the rhino which is not found here, other wildlife in the park include cheetahs, hippos, and rich bird life since the park is an important birding area.
Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, lunch & Dinner)

DAY 7: AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK – NAIROBI
Wake up to the amazing view of the snow- capped Mount Kilimanjaro which stands as the tallest mountain in Africa, after breakfast head for game watching in the fascinating mosaic of swamps, salt licks, acacia forest and grasslands that is food for wildlife. This includes elephants, gazelles, giraffes, zebras, antelopes, buffaloes including many others. In search of the Big 5 (Buffaloes, Elephants, Leopards, Lions but no rhinos) other predators found in the park include the cheetah, hyena and wild dogs. The park is rich with bird species and this is good for bird watching. After lunch we shall head straight for Nairobi marking the END your safari where you will be dropped at the airport or hotel of choice.
