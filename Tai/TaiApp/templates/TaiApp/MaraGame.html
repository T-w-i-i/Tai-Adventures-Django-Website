Arrival in Nairobi
The safari will start immediately you land at the airport where you will be picked up and drop at your hotel of choice, you will have a rest as you feel the beautiful ambiance of the famously known City under the Sun. Briefing with the guide who will share information and familiarizing with what to expect during the safari.

DAY 1 : NAIROBI- MARA GAME RESERVE
Pick up after breakfast at 0800hrs from your hotel and depart straight for Masai Mara Game Reserve. The drive will be full of beautiful sceneries displayed from the historic architecture of buildings within the city and also the beautiful green capture of vegetation along the highway. Through the Rift Valley escarpments at Mai-Mahiu - Narok highway view point. Here you will get to have a spectacular view of the Great Rift-valley and its vast scenery. Along the highway is the Old Roman Catholic church that was built by the Italian prisoners who constructed the highway. Arrive in the Reserve through the Mara Sekenani Gate and check in at our luxury tented camp. We will have an evening game drive in the spectacular game reserve for its display of wildlife viewing and an epic unforgettable moment in such for the Big 5 (Buffalo, Elephant, Lion, Leopard, Rhino). This is also a good time to spot predators hunting for prey since they hunt under low temperatures. Dinner and overnight at a Tented Camp
(Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 2 : FULL DAY GAME DRIVE AT THE MARA RESERVE
Have early preparations for breakfast as you head for a full day game drive, in search for the Big 5. We will get to view a variety of wildlife and bird species with the blend of the fauna within the range of the Mara eco-system. Other species of animals within the park includes the Masaai Giraffe, Thompson gazelle, cheetah, zebras, the wildebeest, hyena etc. The reserve is also home to various species of birds such as the Secretary birds, ostrich, marabou stork etc. Your day will be marked with plenty of game viewing and animal searching. Lunch will be served as picnic during the drive. Dinner and Overnight at a Tented Camp
(Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 3 : MARA RESERVE- NAIROBI
Wake up bell for an early morning game drive in search for predators hunting or feeding after making a kill, return to the hotel for breakfast as we make our return to Nairobi driving through the Game Reserve.
(Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch)

Dropping off will be at your hotel of choice.
