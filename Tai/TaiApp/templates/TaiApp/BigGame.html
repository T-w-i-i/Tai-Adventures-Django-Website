Arrival in Nairobi
The safari will start immediately you land at the airport where you are picked up and dropped at your hotel of choice, you will have a rest as you feel the beautiful ambiance of the famously known City under the Sun. Briefing with the guide who will share information and familiarizing with what to expect during the safari. Nairobi excursion afford upon request.

DAY 1: NAIROBI – MASAAI MARA RESERVE. Departure starts early after breakfast heading for Narok via the Nairobi Mai Mahiu road, along the way you will be able to see the different types of settlement from urban set up to rural houses. Across the road lays vast tea plantations, savanna grasslands and sisal plantation as you approach Narok County the home to wildlife. Arrive in Mara late afternoon for check in and have an evening drive in the reserve on lookout for the Big 5(Buffalo, Elephant, Leopard, Lion and Rhino). Return to the lodge at sunset for dinner with the magnificent sunset on the savanna grassland. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 2: MARA RESERVE FULL DAY DRIVE Early game drive after breakfast across the reserve which is famously known for the wildebeest migration across the Serengeti and Mara Reserve which make it’s the Seventh Wonder of the World. More than 1.3 Million wildebeest migrate making it a kill season for the carnivores including the crocodiles in Mara River. The savanna grassland and plantations of leafy bushes is food for millions ranging from elephants, antelopes, buffaloes, giraffes, wildebeest, zebra and the black rhino. Following these the predators have plenty to hunt and you won’t miss a hunt or meal session from the carnivores which include lions, leopards, cheetahs, hyenas, wild dogs, jackals and foxes. Lunch will be picnic packed lunch at the Mara River as you enjoy the sights and sounds as the migration takes place. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 3: MARA RESERVE FULL DAY DRIVE After breakfast embark for game drive across the vast Mara triangle which is bordered by Ngamia Hills to the East Side with leafy bushes which are liked by black rhinos which are thriving in the reserve. Search for game hunting and in search of preys across the grasslands, the park is also an important birding area which is home to various bird species like vultures, secretary birds, marabou stork, big raptors, kori bustards, and guinea fowl including many others. Have late lunch as picnic in the reserve as you are on lookout for wildlife and return for overnight at the lodge late in the evening. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 4: MASAAI MARA – AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK Late breakfast after which check out from lodge driving through the reserve, with a short stop at the masaai village which we shall do upon your request as we head for Amboseli. It will be a long drive and this gives the best experience for scenic viewing, architectural designs from the rural and urban settlements along the way. Arrive late in the evening for Amboseli for a late game drive if time allows and fatigue from the journey. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 5: AMBOSELI FULL DAY DRIVE Wake up to the amazing view of the snow- capped Mount Kilimanjaro which stands as the tallest mountain in Africa, after breakfast head for game watching in the fascinating mosaic of swamps, salt licks, acacia forest and grasslands that is food for wildlife. This includes elephants, gazelles, giraffes, zebras, antelopes, buffaloes including many others. In search of the Big 5 (Buffaloes, Elephants, Leopards, Lions but no rhinos) other predators include the cheetah, hyena and wild dogs. The park is rich with bird species and this is good for bird watching. Late lunch followed by evening drive after which you break for the night. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 6: AMBOSELI – TSAVO WEST NATIONAL PARK At dawn drive to Tsavo National Park after breakfast arriving late in the afternoon for an evening game drive in the park with the beautiful view of Chyulu Hills and Mount Kilimanjaro. Inside the park are beautiful landmarks such as shetani lava flow that erupted 500 years ago, Poachers Lookout, Ngulia rhino sanctuary and the roaring rocks. The park has a rich history of the famous “Man Eaters of Tsavo” making game viewing interesting in search for the big 5 (Buffaloes, Elephants, Leopards, Lions and Rhinos) making it interesting for the drive and wildlife watching. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 7: TSAVO WEST- TAVO EAST NATIONAL PARK Early morning drive through Tsavo West heading to Tsavo West National Park for a game drive in such for the red elephants of Tsavo and game ranging from lions, leopards, cheetahs, rhinos and zebras including others such as birds and prey. The Lugard water falls from Athi River through the park and the Yatta Plateau one of the world’s longest lava flow and enjoy sundowners with the beautiful scenic view of the Tsavo. From the drive and wildlife watching embark for overnight at the lodge. Dinner & Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 8: TSAVO EAST – NAIROBI Late breakfast as you enjoy the beautiful scenic view of the vast savanna bush land with melodies from the rich birdlife species in the park as you check out of the lodge heading for Nairobi. Arrive in Nairobi late in the evening with drop off at your hotel of choice or airport as this mark the END of the Safari. Meal Plans: (Breakfast & Lunch)
