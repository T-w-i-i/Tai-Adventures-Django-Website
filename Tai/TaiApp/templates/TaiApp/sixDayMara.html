ARRIVAL IN NAIROBI

The safari starts immediately you land in Kenya where you will be picked up by our drivers at the airport and lead you to your hotel of booking. Get enough rest at the hotel after being briefed by our guides on the safari that you are participate in, the do’s and don’ts during the safari and what to expect when in the game drives.

Day 1: Nairobi – Mara Game Reserve
After breakfast you will be picked up at your hotel around 0800hrs, we shall start our departure heading straight to the reserve, with the help of our driver-guides you get to learn about the architectural design from colonial period within the city as you drive through Nairobi City. Pass through the vast landscape of Rift Valley escarpments along the Mai Mahiu highway, with a beautiful scenic display, quick stop over as you take pictures and experience the beauty of the Rift Valley. Along the highway you will come across the ancient Roman Catholic Church that was built by Italians, you will notice the different change of vegetation as you get into Narok . Arrive in the Reserve late in the evening where you will check in the hotel we have booked for you, embark for an evening game drive in search for wildlife. The Mara eco-system is famous for hosting the Big 5 (Buffaloes, Elephants, Leopards, Lions and Rhinos) it is also known for its great wildebeest migrations which makes it a wonder of the world.
Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner
Dinner & Overnight: At Tented Camp

Day 2: Full-Day Game Drive in Mara
Morning game drive starts early at 0600hrs after breakfast, the day will be marked with memorable activities and experience since we will start in search for predators on a hunt. Hunting usually happens in the morning hours when the temperature are down, you will get to see different wildlife such as the Thompson Gazelles, Zebras, Giraffes, Hyenas, Impalas, Jackals, Warthogs and different species of birds like the Ostrich, Secretary Bird, Guinea Fowl, Marabou Stork and the Vultures. Have picnic lunch along the great Mara River as you get to experience the Wildebeest Migration taking place and the intense moment between them and the crocodiles on Mara River. Evening game drive as we head back to the hotel for the night, evening hunts are also common and great chance to see predators looking for a meal.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Dinner & Overnight: At Tented Camp

Day 3: Mara Reserve – Lake Nakuru National Park
Game drive starts early before breakfast driving through the vast vegetation of the reserve with the beautiful melodies of different bird species serenading the environs, we get to search for the big 5 and also other wildlife hidden in the vegetation such as the cheetah the fastest land mammal. Return to the hotel for breakfast and checking out of the hotel as we head for Lake Nakuru National Park, pass through the Masaai Village for a traditional dance and small cultural tourism. Drive through Narok town as we go to Nakuru town; arrive in the evening for check in at hotel where you shall relax for the night from the long drive. Lake Nakuru National park is home to the Flamingos and wildlife such as the rhino, giraffes, zebras, monkeys and different bird species.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Dinner & Overnight: At Tented Camp

Day 4: Nakuru – Amboseli National Park
Early morning game drive after breakfast where you will go looking for predators such as lions, leopards and cheetahs hunting for a meal, visit the lake side with the beautiful flamingos covering the sky pink. Wildlife such as zebras, giraffes and monkeys playing around with the black rhino grazing in its hide outs with the more than 200 bird species in the park the game drive will be marked with a lot of activities. Return to the hotel for late lunch and check out for Amboseli arriving late in the evening.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch& Dinner
Dinner & Overnight: At Tented Camp

Day 5: Full Game Drive
Wake up to the magnificent view of the snowcapped Mount Kilimanjaro, immediately after breakfast head for the full game drive in search for the big 5 and predators hunting. Being a premium park it is home to more than 200 species of birds and well known for hosting elephants which are the heaviest land mammals. Various wildlife are found in the park such as giraffes, zebras, impalas and gazelles herding in the vast plains of the national park, drive through the park and it vegetation as we return to the hotel for the evening.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Dinner & Overnight: At Tented Camp

Day 6: Amboseli – Nairobi
After breakfast you will enjoy an early game drive in the park with the view of Mount Kilimanjaro from the neighboring Tanzania country, from the game drive return to the hotel for lunch and check out from the hotel as we head for Nairobi. The safari ends at your hotel of booking or the airport where you will be dropped by the drive.
