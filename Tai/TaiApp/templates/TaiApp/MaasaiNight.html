This is the shortest well customized safari favorable for business people who are in the Kenya for a short period or those on transit and would like to have the magical feeling experience. Itinerary Despite the safari being short it involves various activities such as scenery viewing along the landscapes of the vast Rift Valley Escarpments which stretches across the East African Countries. Other activities include, bird watching, wildlife viewing through the game drive, and also the great adventure and terrain driving through the game reserve. Finally there is cultural tourism during a stop at the Masaai Village.

Day 1: Nairobi - Masaai Mara
Our safari starts early in the morning immediately you land at the airport or from your hotel of booking. We drive through the famously vast spread Rift Valley escarpments heading to Masai Mara Game Reserve. On the way you get to view the scenic land scape passing through the sorghum plantations along the Mai Mahiu highway arriving in time for lunch. We will have an afternoon game drive till late in the evening looking for the Big 5(Buffalo, elephant, lion, leopard & rhino). Dinner and overnight at tented camp (Meal Plan Lunch & Dinner)

Day 2: Masaai Mara-Nairobi
Early morning game drive in search for the carnivores as they like to hunt in the morning hours when the temperature are low, you will get to see other wildlife like the giraffe, zebras, wildebeest, Thompson gazelles, hyenas, ostriches, foxes and birds in the vast eco-system. Depart after breakfast for Nairobi through the park enjoying the landscape of the Game Reserve. Drop off at your hotel of choice or transfer to the airport for onward flight home. Meal plan: (Breakfast & lunch)
