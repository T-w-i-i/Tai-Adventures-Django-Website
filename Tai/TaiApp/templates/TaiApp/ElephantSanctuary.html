Arrival in Nairobi
The safari will start immediately you land at the airport where you will be picked up and drop at your hotel of choice, you will have a rest as you feel the beautiful ambiance of the famously known City under the Sun. Briefing with the guide who will share information and familiarizing with what to expect during the safari. Nairobi excursion afford upon request.

DAY 1: NAIROBI – AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK
Safari starts immediately after breakfast from your hotel where you will be picked up by our driver guide and depart straight for Amboseli using the Nairobi – Mombasa Road via Emali. Along the way you will notice the change in structural settings and designs as you go further from the city, passing through different towns and counties. Arrive at Amboseli late in the afternoon for lunch after which you shall have an evening game drive in the park which is named from a Masaai word which means “salt dust”. The park is a big game paradise with lions, leopards, cheetah, hippos, and buffalo including species of birds but no rhinos are found here. Head back to the camp/lodge at sunset. Dinner and Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 2: FULL DAY GAME DRIVE AT AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK
Being on the foothills of Mount Kilimanjaro (in Tanzania) you will be able to view the snowcapped mountain in the early morning sunset as you have breakfast. It’s the tallest standing mountain in Africa and has mosaic swamps of salt licks, a dry salt lick with acacia forest and grassland. This diverse land is home to African Bush Elephant which you will not miss as you drive through the park in search for game. Other wildlife in the park includes the impalas, zebras, giraffe, ostrich, gazelles and different bird species which you shall come across during the game drive. Lunch will be packed picnic which you shall have late in the afternoon. Dinner and Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 3: AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK – TSAVO WEST NATIONAL PARK
Early morning drive as in search for game hunting for prey and eating from a successful hunt, here the beautiful melodies from the birds of Amboseli will keep you entertained. Have late breakfast after which you shall head for Tsavo West National Park for an evening game drive. The park is home to elephants and other game; you won’t miss to find a herd of elephant every kilometer you drive, other game including the big 5 ( lion, buffalo, rhino, leopard and elephants) and cheetahs. Dinner and Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plan: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 4: FULL DAY GAME DRIVE AT TSAVO WEST NATIONAL PARK
With the early morning views of Mount Kilimanjaro and Chyulu hills you get to have breakfast with this breathtaking scenery, followed by a drive into the park which has landmarks such as the Shetani lava flow from Chyulu Hills that erupted 500 year ago. Poachers Lookout and Ngulia Rhino Sanctuary, Tsavo fabled red earth, the Roaring rocks and the Mzima springs are other landmarks in the park. With its rich history of the “Man Eaters of Tsavo” you will come across prides of lion basking under a tree from the hot sun of Taveta. Other wildlife in the park includes the lesser kudu, zebras, giraffes, mongoose, dik dik and porcupines. Water animals such as crocodiles and hippos with 600 species of birds are in the park. Dinner and Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plan: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 5: FULL DAY GAME DRIVE AT TSAVO EAST NATIONAL PARK Early breakfast before dawn followed by a drive to Tsavo East National Park an extension from Tsavo West National Park. Famously known as the “Theatre of Wildlife” you will come across herds of red dust elephants, rhinos and buffalos in the dry vegetation of Tsavo. The animal view point where wildlife such as elephants, buffalos, zebras, giraffes and other prey come to quench their thirst and where predators like lions like for hunting will be our stop over point. Also in the park are birds such as the ostrich and 500 species of birds flying in the vast skies of Tsavo. Dinner and Overnight at Lodge
Meal Plan: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 6: TSAVO EAST NATIONAL PARK – MOMBASA
After breakfast there will be no game drive, you will drive straight for Mombasa where you will arrive in the afternoon and check in the hotel for lunch with a view of the beach as you relax from the long drive. You shall visit the Old Town with its rich architecture designs from early 1900s influenced by the Indians and Arabs. Visit the Mandhry Mosque one of the oldest mosques used in the 1570/1571 as we walk to the Old Harbor making stop over at the curios shop. Dinner and Overnight at hotel
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

Day 7: MONUMENT VISIT AND BEACH LIFE
From a late breakfast head for Fort Jesus (A Cape of Good Hope) which is a world heritage site and built in the shape of a human being, being a stopover trading point for Persians, Europeans, Arabs and Portuguese. It has the display of early cannons used by the British during war and was home to 400 soldiers with rooms like the Captain Watch room, Gun platform and Portuguese church. Visit the butterfly house as you drive back to the hotel for lunch preparing for the beach experience at Mombasa Marine National Park.

Day 8: MOMBASA MARINE NATIONAL PARK
With its sky blue water surface which are good for activities like wind surfing, water skiing and snorkeling with deep sea diving in search for marine life like the star fish, stone fish, crabs, cucumber sea urchins, turtles and coral reef the sea has rich and memorable activities to engage in. The sandy beach is good for sand bathing and beach sports such as football and volleyball, your day will be marked with a lot of activities. Night life is also encouraged when there are the annual cultural festivals in display of different traditional cultures and music including dances i.e. the Dugong Festival held in Lamu on the month of May and the Mombasa Carnival held in the month of November. Dinner and Overnight at hotel
Meal Plans: (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)

DAY 9: MOMBASA TO NAIROBI
After late breakfast check out headed for Nairobi where you shall be drop off at the airport/hotel marking the end of your safari with us. Thank You for choosing to travel with us.
